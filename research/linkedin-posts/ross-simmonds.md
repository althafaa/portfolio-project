**Link:**

**Summary:** 
Ross Simmonds argues that marketers are optimizing for the visible internet while ignoring the layer that AI systems actually use to generate recommendations. He explains that websites, design, and copywriting influence human perception, while AI systems rely on deeper signals such as authority, mentions, citations, and structured information.

**Key Takeaway:** 
Modern brands must optimize for both human audiences and AI systems. Visibility in AI-generated recommendations increasingly depends on machine-readable trust and authority signals rather than website design alone.
