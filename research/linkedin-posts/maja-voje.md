**Summary:** 
Maja Voje shares lessons from Mark Roberge, the founding CRO of HubSpot, on one of the most common startup mistakes: scaling at the wrong pace. Companies often either scale too early before achieving product-market fit or scale too slowly after finding growth opportunities.
