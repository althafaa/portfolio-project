**Link:** 
https://www.linkedin.com/posts/katebour_how-do-you-sell-a-product-that-solves-a-problem-activity-7467898892538417153-PhyA

**Summary:** 
Kate Bour explains how to sell a product that solves a problem customers may not realize they have. She argues that customers often search for solutions to symptoms, not root problems, so marketers need to package the message around what customers already know they want while delivering what they actually need.

**Key Takeaway:** 
Effective messaging starts with the symptom customers already feel, then reframes it into the deeper problem your product solves.

**Theme:**
Product Positioning, Messaging Strategy, Demand Generation, Problem Awareness, B2B SaaS Marketing

**Content Framework:**
Visible symptom
↓
Customer searches for symptom-level solution
↓
Marketer understands root problem
↓
Message is packaged around known desire
↓
Product delivers the deeper solution

**Why This Post Is Valuable:**
1. Strong fit for B2B SaaS positioning and demand generation.
2. Useful for LinkedIn organic strategy because content should begin with pain the audience already recognizes.
3. Helps marketers avoid overly abstract messaging.
4. Provides a practical framework for turning attention into demand.
