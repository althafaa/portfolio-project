↓

↓
Sales performance improves

No strong brand
↓

↓

↓
Same playbook may perform worse

**Why This Post Is Valuable:**
1. Strong fit for B2B SaaS founder and sales strategy.
2. Connects brand strength with sales performance.
3. Useful for understanding why LinkedIn organic brand-building can support sales.
4. Provides practical advice for evaluating sales talent.
