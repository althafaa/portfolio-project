## Title: The 0.01% Strategy: How Fractional CMOs are Using AI to Make 20x More
## URL: https://youtu.be/Rnsy-aEEOgs?si=R95BxHRBqYwOVRYi

**Key Insights:**
**1. Marketing leadership is becoming more flexible**
The traditional full-time CMO role can be expensive and risky, especially for growing companies. Fractional CMOs give companies access to senior strategy without committing to a full-time executive cost.

**2. The fractional CMO role is about orchestration, not doing everything**
Strong fractional CMOs do not personally execute every channel. They focus on positioning, strategy, priorities, and managing the right mix of specialists, agencies, tools, and workflows.

**3. AI increases leverage for solo marketing leaders**
AI agents, LLMs, and automated workflows allow one senior marketer to operate with much more scale. The best fractional CMOs will not just “use AI,” but build systems where AI supports research, content, analysis, execution, and coordination.
