# Video YouTube

## Title: ABM: What Ramp, Snowflake, and Hightouch are doing in 2026
## URL: https://youtu.be/q_PvrQsWu-E?si=ymqZexeDPC4e8Jm2

**Key Insights:**
**1. ABM is sales strategy delivered through marketing**
ABM is not just running ads to target accounts. The strongest version starts with sales alignment: choosing the right accounts, understanding account priorities, and using marketing channels to support sales progression.

**2. High-touch ABM is becoming more important than ad-heavy ABM**
The panel suggests that paid social alone is often overrated for ABM awareness. In-person dinners, niche events, direct mail, one-to-one landing pages, and lunch-and-learns can create stronger engagement, especially in mid-to-late funnel deals.

**3. ABM success should be measured by pipeline movement, not just attribution**
Instead of arguing over which channel gets credit, better teams look at control groups, engagement, pipeline velocity, and sales progression. The goal is not just awareness, but helping target accounts move closer to revenue.
