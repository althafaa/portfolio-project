# Video YouTube

## Title: This Italian startup makes $1.5B a year reviving zombie apps
## URL: https://youtu.be/6kLnodwtGh8?si=oR_ZacTDEcknOLl1

**Key Insights:**
**1. Bending Spoons wins by buying under-optimized products, not starting from zero**
Their strategy is to acquire products with existing brand recognition and loyal users, then improve monetization, operations, and product quality.

**2. The company acts like an AI-powered product repair factory**
After acquisition, Bending Spoons uses specialized teams, proprietary tools, and AI infrastructure to diagnose problems, reduce costs, improve features, and optimize growth.
This is important for B2B SaaS because it shows that competitive advantage can come from operational systems, not just product ideas.

**3. Control and long-term ownership are central to the model**
Bending Spoons bootstrapped for years, reinvested profits, and later used debt financing instead of relying heavily on early venture capital. This helped them maintain control and follow a “hold forever” strategy.
