## Title: Why Emotional Targeting Matters Even More in B2B
## URL: https://youtu.be/LkPAhYkZ-7k?si=6CWM6g3prNJ4HQ1q

**Key Insights:**
**1. B2B buyers are emotional, not purely rational**
B2B marketing often assumes buyers only care about ROI, features, and efficiency. Talia Wolf argues the opposite: B2B decisions are deeply emotional because they involve professional risk.

**2. The real buying reason is often hidden**
Good messaging starts by researching why customers really buy, not just what they say on the surface.

**3. B2B SaaS messaging should reduce risk and build confidence**
Buyers worry about choosing the wrong tool, wasting budget, damaging credibility, or making their team look bad. Strong messaging should make them feel safer, clearer, and more confident.
