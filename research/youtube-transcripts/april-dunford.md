# Video YouTube

## Title: Post Positioning - Testing and Execution
## URL: https://youtu.be/D9pf_iEomjY?si=NQX7fJ58KIgva8WE

**Key Insights:**
**1. Positioning should be tested in sales conversations, not only on the homepage**
April argues that homepage A/B tests are too noisy for testing positioning. A better method is to train strong sales reps on the new pitch and test it with best-fit prospects.

**2. A strong pitch needs a clear structure**
The sales pitch should not be random. It should move through a logical story:

context
↓
market category
↓
differentiated value
↓
proof
↓
call to action

This helps prospects understand not only what the product does, but why it matters and why it is different.

**3. Positioning needs consistency after the workshop**
After positioning work, teams should create a messaging document before updating assets. This becomes the source of truth for sales, marketing, campaigns, events, and website copy.
