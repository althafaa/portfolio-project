# Key Findings

After analyzing LinkedIn content from 9 B2B experts:

## Finding 1

Strong opinions outperform neutral educational content.

## Finding 2

Frameworks are the most common content format.

## Finding 3

AI is becoming a dominant topic across nearly all experts.

## Finding 4

The most successful posts focus on a single idea.

## Finding 5

Personal experiences are used to build authority.

---

# Recommendations

For future B2B LinkedIn content:

1. Lead with a strong hook.
2. Focus on one insight per post.
3. Support claims with data or experience.
4. Use frameworks whenever possible.
5. Prioritize clarity over complexity.
