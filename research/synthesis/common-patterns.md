# Common Patterns Across Experts

## Pattern 1: Strong Contrarian Hooks

**Person:**
- Justin Welsh
- Chris Walker
- Dave Gerhardt

**Observation:**
Top creators start with tension, disagreement, or a surprising statement.

**Implication:**
Future content should lead with a strong opinion.

## Pattern 2: Framework-Based Writing

**Person:**
- Amanda Natividad
- Ross Simmonds
- Maja Voje

**Observation:**
Most successful B2B posts use frameworks.

**Implication:**
Content should package ideas into repeatable systems.

## Pattern 3: Positioning Before Execution

**Person:**
- April Dunford
- Maja Voje
- Katelyn Bourgoin

**Observation:**
These experts emphasize that marketing execution only works after the company is clear on customer, category, value, and differentiation.

**Implication:**
Before scaling LinkedIn content, ads, or campaigns, define the positioning foundation first.
